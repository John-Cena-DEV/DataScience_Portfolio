1. Patient Demographics Analysis

"Most patients (45%) are in the 30–39 age group, indicating the core customer base."

Business Recommendation:

Target marketing campaigns and educational content specifically to the 30-39 year old segment.

2. Case Type & Grafts Analysis

"Average grafts increase steadily with patient age..."

Business Recommendation:

Use age-based graft averages for more accurate inventory planning and surgical cost estimations.

4. Patient Result Distribution

"Patients with >3500 grafts are 20% more likely to report ‘Good Result’..."

Business Recommendation:

Use graft count data during pre-surgery counseling to set realistic patient expectations.

3. Branch Patient Distribution

"Haridwar branch attracts 3-4 times more patients than Delhi in the core under-40 age groups."

Business Recommendation:

Investigate Haridwar's success in attracting younger demographics and apply learnings to Delhi's marketing strategy.

5. Follow-up Compliance Analysis

"Retention drops sharply after Month 3. While 45% attend the 1st follow-up, none visited after 7 Months."

Business Recommendation:

Introduce automated SMS/WhatsApp reminders starting from Month 3. Offer small incentives for completing all 12 follow-ups to improve long-term engagement.

6. Monthly Surgery Trends

"Monthly surgeries peaked in January, declined to a low in March, then dropped sharply in August."

Business Recommendation:

Analyze the factors contributing to the sharp decline in August to prevent similar drops in the future.
